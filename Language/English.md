## 12. KEY GRAMMAR RULES TO REMEMBER

### Question Formation

**Wrong Pattern:** You are coming? / Where you are going? **Correct Pattern:** Are you coming? / Where are you going?

**Rule:** In questions, put helping verb (is/are/do/does/have/has) BEFORE the subject

### State Verbs (Never use -ing form)

❌ I am loving / I am wanting / I am knowing / I am having (possession) ✅ I love / I want / I know / I have

**Common state verbs:** love, like, hate, want, need, know, understand, believe, think (opinion), have (possession)

### Since vs For

**Since** = Starting point (since Monday, since 2020, since morning) **For** = Duration (for 3 days, for 2 hours, for a week)

### Present Perfect vs Simple Past

**Use Simple Past** when you mention WHEN something happened:

- I ate lunch at 1 PM
- I went there yesterday
- She called me last night

**Use Present Perfect** when you DON'T mention when:

- I have eaten lunch
- I have been there
- She has called me

## 1. TALKING WITH COLLEAGUES (Male/Female)

### Project Discussions - Full Conversations

**Scenario 1: Asking for help with a bug**

❌ **Wrong Conversation:**

- You: "Hey, you are free? I am stuck from 2 hours in this bug."
- Colleague: "What happened?"
- You: "The API is not working from morning. You can help me?"

✅ **Correct Conversation:**

- You: "Hey, are you free? I've been stuck on this bug for 2 hours."
- Colleague: "Sure, what's going on?"
- You: "The API hasn't been working since morning. Can you help me?"
- Colleague: "Yeah, let me take a look. What error are you getting?"
- You: "It's throwing a 500 error. I've tried restarting the server but it didn't help."

**मराठी:**

- तू: "अरे, तू फ्री आहेस का? मी 2 तासांपासून या बगवर अडकलो आहे."
- सहकारी: "काय झालं?"
- तू: "API सकाळपासून काम करत नाहीये. तू मला मदत करशील का?"

---

**Scenario 2: Status update in standup**

❌ **Wrong:**

- Manager: "What you are working on?"
- You: "I am working on login feature from yesterday. I am completing it by today."

✅ **Correct:**

- Manager: "What are you working on?"
- You: "I've been working on the login feature since yesterday. I'll complete it by today."
- Manager: "Any blockers?"
- You: "No, everything is going smoothly."

**मराठी:**

- मॅनेजर: "तू कशावर काम करतोयस?"
- तू: "मी कालपासून लॉगिन फीचरवर काम करतोय. मी ते आजपर्यंत पूर्ण करेन."

**Scenario 3: Taking leave**

❌ **Wrong:**

- You: "Sir, I am not feeling well from morning. Can I take leave?"

- You: "I am having headache and fever."

✅ **Correct:**

- You: "Hi, I'm not feeling well. Can I take the day off?"

- You: "I've had a headache and fever since this morning."
- Boss: "No problem, take rest. Hope you feel better soon."

**मराठी:**

- तू: "सर, मला बरं वाटत नाहीये. मी रजा घेऊ का?"
- बॉस: "काय झालं?"

## 9. QUICK GRAMMAR REMINDERS

### Question Formation Pattern

**Always use:** Helping verb + Subject + Main verb?

- Are you coming?
- Did you eat?
- Have you finished?
- Can you help?
- Will you go?

### State Verbs - Never use -ing

❌ Wrong: I am loving, I am knowing, I am understanding ✅ Correct: I love, I know, I understand

### Since vs For

**Since** = Starting point → since Monday, since 2020, since morning **For** = Duration → for 3 days, for 2 hours, for a week
